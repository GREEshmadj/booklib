ABOUT





BookSwap: Connecting Book Lovers



BookSwap is an innovative platform designed for avid readers who want to share their love for books.
Whether you're a bibliophile, a casual reader, or just someone looking to declutter their bookshelf,
BookSwap offers a seamless way to exchange books with like-minded individuals.


How It Works:



1. User Profiles and Authentication:


When users sign up, they provide their Aadhaar card details for identity verification.
We prioritize privacy and security, ensuring that personal information remains confidential.
No actual money changes hands; it's all about fostering a sense of community and promoting reading.

2. Listing Books and Setting Radius:



Users create profiles and list the books they're willing to exchange.
They can set their preferred exchange radius (e.g., within 10 miles of their location).
BookSwap uses geolocation to match users within the specified radius.

3. Points-Based System:



Instead of traditional currency, we use a points system.
Lending a book earns users points, while borrowing deducts points.
This encourages fair exchanges and active participation.

Book Requests and Notifications:



Users can browse available books and request exchanges.
Our notification system alerts users when someone requests their book or proposes a swap.
All communication happens through our secure messaging platform.

5. Contact Sharing:



Once a swap is agreed upon, users can share contact details.
We recommend using discretion and respecting privacy.
Users can connect via phone calls to coordinate the exchange.
